Lasagna

This is a lasagna with cheese and tomato sauce. Served with leafy green salad and some garlic bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh prasley
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 package shredded mozzarella
  1. Gather all of the ingredients.
  2. Combine pork and ground beed in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
